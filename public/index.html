Biography

I am a clinical pharmacist with a PhD in the philosophy of science. My research focusses on philosophy of science, philosophy of medicine and ethics. I am particularly interested in exploring philosophical approaches to challenges that arise in interpreting and applying biomedical research.

I am also a member of a group of researchers in the School of Pharmacy working on better understanding medication adherence.

I teach professional practice, therapeutics, clinical epidemiology, critical appraisal and ethics.
